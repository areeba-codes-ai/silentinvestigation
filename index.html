Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is private investigation legal in Pakistan?+
Yes. Private investigation is a lawful profession in Pakistan when conducted within legal boundaries. Silent Watch strictly adheres to all applicable laws — we never engage in illegal surveillance, hacking or any unlawful activities.
How confidential is my case?+
Your case is 100% confidential. We never share client information, case details or findings with any third party. All communication is securely handled and records are maintained with the highest privacy standards.
How quickly can you start working on my case?+
Most cases begin within 24 hours of consultation. Urgent cases receive same-day or next-morning deployment depending on case type and location. We understand time is often critical.
Do you operate outside Lahore?+
Yes. While our headquarters are in Lahore, we operate across Punjab and handle cases in major cities throughout Pakistan. Contact us to discuss your specific location and needs.
What does the final report include?+
Your report includes documented findings, a factual summary, evidence collected (photographs, records, etc.) and a professional written assessment — all delivered securely and in strict confidence.
How much do your services cost?+
Pricing varies by case complexity, duration and nature. We offer a free initial consultation with a transparent quote — no hidden fees, no surprises. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.
